Live music Across Three Stages

From some of the best local musicians, including: Arcadia Roots, Sam Draisey, The Fairweather Riders and Chloe Mogg. Plus, top young bands such as Broken Wires, Losa and Camouflage Confusion and a special Schools showcase.

The day will end with a unique performance from the Mothership!

Sustainability

Talks, exhibitions, stalls and demonstrations on how to live more sustainably in a changing world, including anappearance by the inspiring young environmentalist Mya-Rose Craig “Bird Girl”who has over 60,000 followers online.

Hands-On Workshops

With something for everyone, including African Drumming, the Wild Earth Movement with sustainable activities for children, Painting workshops and creating the Climate Change Graffiti Wall with nationally renowned artists.

Our Solar Powered Mindfulness area

Our festival café set in the school grounds arboretum, with readings fromTolkien, a Song-Writing workshop, Big Cat Yoga, Sea Shanty workshops and Open Mic sessions hosted by Ben and Stu Green.

Street Food Court and Licensed Bar

Local food and drink at it's finest. The bar hosted by Take a Sip will include real ales from Bewdley Brewery, and local ciders and spirits.

The Local Craft Bazaar

Featuring some of our local trading heroes, peruse the stalls at our craft bazaar and be inspired by human creativity.

Kids Bouncy Play Area

Hosted by Quads n Castles, our bouncy castle play area will be a fun way to let off all that energy.
